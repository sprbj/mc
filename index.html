About Us

Souparnika Projects is a leading construction company based in Kerala, providing high-quality residential, commercial, industrial, and infrastructure development services across India. With over 25 years of experience, we are committed to delivering excellence in every project we undertake.

Our Sectors

Residential Construction

We specialize in building dream homes with quality craftsmanship and attention to detail. Our residential projects are designed to provide comfort and style, tailored to the unique needs of each client.

Commercial Construction

Our commercial projects include office buildings, retail spaces, and more, tailored to business needs. We focus on creating functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces that enhance business operations.

Industrial Construction

We handle industrial construction projects with a focus on durability and functionality. Our work includes the construction of factories, warehouses, and other industrial facilities, ensuring they meet operational needs.

Infrastructure Development

Our infrastructure projects include road construction, bridges, and other essential public works. We are dedicated to developing infrastructure that supports community growth and enhances connectivity.

Current Projects

AVT Natural Products Limited

At AVT Natural Products Limited, we are primarily focused on the maintenance of plants and various construction activities. Our work involves ensuring the smooth operation and upkeep of their facilities to support their natural product operations.

Kerala Forest Department

For the Kerala Forest Department, our efforts are directed towards the construction of office buildings and their maintenance. This project is crucial for providing functional and well-maintained facilities to support forest management and administrative functions.

Nest Private Limited

At Nest Private Limited, we are engaged in constructing new buildings and maintaining existing ones. Our role is to enhance the company’s infrastructure and ensure its facilities are in top condition to support its operations.

Kerala Public Works Department (PWD)

Our work with the Kerala Public Works Department focuses on road construction and building projects. This includes improving and maintaining roadways and constructing various public buildings to support the department’s infrastructure development goals.
